About "Send A Smile"

"Send A Smile" is an app developed on Google App Engine. The app allows you to send little acts of encouragement to each other through the use of "Smiles" and Virtual Gifts. The app was created to motivate people to go on with life and keep on smiling no matter how tough the world gets.

Users may make use of the app in these scenarios:

  • Sending Birthday Wishes
  • Best Wishes for Students Going for Competitions
  • Encourage Friends to Be Happy
